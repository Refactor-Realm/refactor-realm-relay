## ⏱ Uptime & Availability

This relay operates on a **hybrid model** designed for reliability, low cost, and long-term sustainability.

### 🟢 Daily Online Hours
The relay is fully online and accepting writes during the following window:

**10:00 AM – 10:00 PM Eastern Time (EST/EDT)**  
(With natural day-to-day variation of ±30–60 minutes.)

During these hours:
- Full WebSocket connectivity is available  
- All writes are accepted  
- Performance is highest  
- Local relay storage is active  

### 🌙 Nighttime Behavior (Hybrid Model)
Outside the daily operating window, the relay transitions into **Worker Gateway Mode**:

- The public endpoint remains online  
- Stripe billing and webhook processing remain active  
- Users receive a friendly “offline” message  
- No writes are accepted during this period  

This ensures the relay is **always reachable**, even when the local node is offline.

### 🧩 Why a Hybrid Model?
This approach provides:

- **Lower cost** (no VPS required)  
- **Predictable uptime** during peak Nostr activity  
- **A stable public endpoint** 24/7  
- **Unlimited writes** during active hours  
- **A sustainable path to growth**  

As the subscriber base grows, the relay will transition to a **24/7 dedicated environment** with multi-region redundancy.

### 📈 Roadmap Toward 24/7 Uptime
- Migration to dedicated hardware or VPS  
- Multi-region Worker routing  
- Optional paid read access  
- Automated failover  
- Public uptime dashboard  

Subscribers will be notified before any major upgrades.
